Subify. Your favourite sandwich filling site, on a mission to make sandwiches fun again!

To expand the site, we've made an issue tracker site. Subify users can request bugs to be fixed and new features to enhance the user experience.

How does it work?

Users can post bugs or tickets, and both bug requests and feature requests can be upvoted. Hopefully all requests will be implemented, but upvotes increase priority - the more upvotes a bug or feature request has, the sooner it will be fixed/added to the site.

Bug tickets and upvotes are free, but feature tickets and upvotes are Â£10.

What counts as a bug/feature?

A bug is an error that produces an incorrect or undesired result - something that prevents the site from working as intended.
A feature can be anything new and exciting that you think would improve the site; a better way of doing things, a new function on top of adding, editing and deleting recipes or a way to share recipes to social media or by messaging.

It's also worth noting that you can only buy one feature ticket/upvote at a time, although there's no limit for how many feature tickets or feature upvotes you can buy.

Contact

If you have any questions or issues beyond described on this page, don't hesitate to contact us by email on info.issuetracker@subify.com.
